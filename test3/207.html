Browsing in the Fine Arts Library

Map collection

"I'm a freshman, so I'm new here, but what I've learned so far is that Olin has one of the best map collections in the country."

Individual study rooms in Mann

"A study room is a nice, cozy, compact environment that's perfect for getting your work done without distractions. It's like your own personal library."

- George Osae '11, CALS

Extensive resources

"As someone who listens to 'Fresh Air' on NPR, what I love about the library is that every time I hear an author or a book that I'm interested in, I can get it right away -- either here in the library, or from Borrow Direct or Interlibrary Loan. The library provides immediate gratification."

- Barbara Berger Eden, Director of Preservation and Collection Management

Olin Media Center

"Did you know that you can take microforms, microfilm, microfiche and microprint and read, print, scan or e-mail them to yourself -- all from Olin's Media Center? We offer photomicrographic machines that can creat PDFs directly, and printing costs money but the scanning is free."

- Suzanne Schwartz, Olin Media Student Supervisor

Manndible

Olin's DVD collection

"I love taking out DVDs from the bottom floor of Olin -- whenever I want to watch a movie, I just go there. I especially like the foreign film selection and loads of interesting documentaries, as well as comedies and TV shows. In the winter, I probably go at least once a week or once every two weeks.

The library mindset
