Employment

Staff (non-academic) and Librarian positions are posted on the Cornell University Jobs web page. Hiring supervisors will receive applications electronically and will collect them until they feel they have a sufficient pool of applicants. The hiring supervisor will contact job candidates for an interview if they feel the qualifications are in-line with their requirements.

If you have questions regarding the status of your application, please contact Lyndsi Prignon at lra8@cornell.edu.
